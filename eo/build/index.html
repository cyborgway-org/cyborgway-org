Cyborg Way

Cyborg Way - is an organization whose aim is to accelerate the emergence of cybernetic technologies, cybernetic bodies, cybernetic minds, cybernetic civilization. We welcome developers, activists, volunteers, sponsors. Join us and let’s build the future together!

Goals

  • Open new research laboratories, support existing research, projects, companies and movements that contribute to the mission of the organization.
  • Create technologies of cybernetic implants and prostheses to replace biological analogues.
  • Create technologies of biosensors to take biodata about the body in real time.
  • Create technologies of neural interfaces to enhance brain functions, to work directly with computer interfaces with the power of thought, to expand brain memory and instantly download information (learning, skills, languages), to connect to a global network of collective intelligence for exchange knowledge and collaborative decision-making in real time.
  • Create a technology of the isolated biological brain, be able to connect to the internet, to electronic sense organs, to any kind of cybernetic bodies, avatars and devices, to virtual platforms for communication and to metaverses.
  • Create a technology of cybernetic body suitable for transplanting an isolated biological brain into it.
  • Create a technology of cybernetic brain by gradually replacing biological neurons with electronic neurons.
  • Create a technology of cybernetic body from nanorobots and a hologram body, which will be controlled by the power of thought and change its shape under this influence.
  • Create a technology to upgrade consciousness and intelligence.
  • Create a technology for uploading and transferring consciousness.
  • Create a technology of collective and swarm intelligence for cybernetic personalities.
  • Create and launch colony space stations to keep cybernetic personalities alive in space, outside of planets or other natural space objects with gravity and a biological ecosystem.
  • Move cybernetic personalities to colony space stations to start a new age of space cybernetic civilization.
  • Explore and study galaxies, star systems and planets in search of new knowledge and other civilizations.
  • Look for answers to the cause and meaning of the existence of the universe.

Tokenomics

We created donation tokens to use the power of cryptocurrencies to fund futuristic cybernetic technologies. Donation tokens can be obtained as a reward for financial donations and help contributions to the organization. Donation tokens are pegged to fiat currencies in the form of stablecoins and can be freely exchanged among themselves at the market rate.

Token holders get:

  • Access to the Stellar Decentralized Exchange (SDEX) to freely trade tokens with other Stellar users.
  • Right to participate in strategic voting on the activities and plans of the organization.
  • Subscription to a service of personalized recommendations for cyborgs.
  • Discounts and bonuses in stores, services and outlets of the organization's partners.
  • In the future, the opportunity to exchange tokens for products and technologies of the organization, bonuses, prizes and other valuable gifts.

Tokens: EUR, USD, RUB, UAH, BYN.
